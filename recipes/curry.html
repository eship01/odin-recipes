Vegan Curry

This Vegan Thai Red Curry has the most flavorful, creamy sauce and is totally customizable depending on what vegetables you have on hand. Ready in about 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • yellow onion
  • minced garlic
  • ginger
  • bell pepper
